About My Final Site

This site was made to do a dedicated breakdown of one of my favorite video games, Counter-Strike: Global Offensive. In this site, you will be able to learn about the the game modes and weapons that the game has to offer. You'll also be able to build your own loadout as if in game. The majority of the information used to detail the game was gathered from the official wikia page: CS:GO Official Wikia Page.
